The Leopard Gecko

Leopard geckos originate from the desserts of Afghanistan, Iran, Pakistan, India, and Nepal. During the 1970's, they became the most common pet lizard to be bred in the United states. Namarri is the name of my leopard gecko. A friend of mine could no longer take care her and was given to me. I had been thinking of getting one soon, it was perfect.
