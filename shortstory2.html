THE BLACKWATER TRIP

About an hour away, there is a river called blackwater. A couple friends and I decided to go tubing down this river. We did not know what was in store for us.

LOOKING BACK

Although this trip was absolutly miserable, it was an experience. An experience that I can share with you guys and maybe my kids one day. It taught me a lot of valuable lessons and made me much more appreciative that I don't live on a river.
